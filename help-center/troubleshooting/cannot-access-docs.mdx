---
title: Cannot Access Documentation Space
description: What to do if your docs space won't load.
---

## Symptoms

- Docs space is blank or shows an error
- Unable to view or edit documents

## Common Causes

- Internet issues
- Account permissions
- Temporary server error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection">
    ````bash
    ping docs.deepikanallana.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log Out and In Again">
    Sign out from your account and log back in to refresh access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
